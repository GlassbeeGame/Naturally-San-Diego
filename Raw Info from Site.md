Naturally San Diego is the  
community for the natural  
product industry in San Diego.

## **We bring together entrepreneurs, investors, brands, retailers and industry experts for one purpose: to make San Diego the best place to launch and scale a natural products company. Join us!**

**Video production credit: [Greg Helton](https://www.linkedin.com/in/greghelton84/) & [Larimore Production Company](https://www.larimoreproductioncompany.com/)**

# Our goal is simple: support the CPG leaders of tomorrow.

Our mission: We aim to make greater San Diego the new nexus of natural products entrepreneurship.

**Video production credit: [Greg Helton](https://www.linkedin.com/in/greghelton84/) & [Larimore Production Company](https://www.larimoreproductioncompany.com/)**

[BECOME A MEMBER](https://naturallysandiego.org/members/)

# What we do

We’re not just another trade organization, we want to make dramatic changes in our CPG ecosystem and provide networking, education and resources for our members to illuminate a path for sustainable growth and ongoing success.

## Our Values:

Cultivating an inclusive community  
Maintaining transparency with all stakeholders  
Empowering members and staff  
Cultivating a continual curiosity  
Remaining focused on collaboration  
Fostering creativity and the entrepreneurial spirit  
Building all aspects of the organization with goal of creating health & harmony between people & the planet, while maintaining healthy bottom line

# ANNUAL EVENTS

Naturally San Diego organizes and hosts a handful of local San Diego events every year. Each event has begun to take on a life of its own with tremendous community enthusiasm and support.

# PITCH SLAM

Naturally San Diego’s Pitch Slam gives natural products entrepreneurs the stage to pitch their up-and-coming companies to a panel of experts and 350 audience members, ultimately winning a suite of prizes from our local San Diego community to help propel their industry growth.

In 2024, we had over 35 applicants and our screening committee narrowed it down to six finalists. Those finalists pitched live, on-stage to a panel of subject-matter expert judges and an audience of 350. The finalists competed against one another for a prize package worth more than $100,000 in product and services.

Join us in Spring 2025 for our next Pitch Slam!

# Find the Best Natural and Organic Products Jobs

Members and Sponsors have access to our national Naturally Network Job Board, powered by [ForceBrands](https://forcebrands.com/). This is the national job board focusing on natural and organic product industry roles and reaches San Diego’s most dynamic network of CPG professionals and supporters.

**Sponsors and Members can:**

- post free job listings and view applications and hiring activity with real-time dashboards
- cross-post to ForceBrands, the largest CPG-specialized job board, at a 20% partner discount
- sync your applicant tracking system (ATS) to the Naturally Network job board so that jobs automatically populate to our network of over 5,000 members

Thank you for exploring and sharing opportunities in our community!

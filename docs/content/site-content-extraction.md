## 2. About / Our Story

### Hero

We're building a more sustainable future.

### Goal / Mission

Our goal is simple: support the CPG leaders of tomorrow.

Our mission: We aim to make greater San Diego the new nexus of natural products entrepreneurship.

### What We Do

We're not just another trade organization, we want to make dramatic changes in our CPG ecosystem and provide networking, education and resources for our members to illuminate a path for sustainable growth and ongoing success.

### Our Values

- Cultivating an inclusive community
- Maintaining transparency with all stakeholders
- Empowering members and staff
- Cultivating a continual curiosity
- Remaining focused on collaboration
- Fostering creativity and the entrepreneurial spirit
- Building all aspects of the organization with goal of creating health & harmony between people & the planet, while maintaining healthy bottom line

### Naturally Network

Naturally San Diego is part of the larger Naturally Network. The Job Board is the "Naturally Network Job Board" — national in scope, reaching a network of over 5,000 members. The Naturally Network has chapters in San Diego and other cities.

The organization launched in mid-2020. The first blog post was June 2, 2020 ("Hello San Diego!"). The first Executive Director (full-time) was Kierstin Rielly, hired at the one-year anniversary in July 2021. Current Executive Director is Renee Solares.

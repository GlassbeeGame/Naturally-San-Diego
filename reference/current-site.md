## Identity & Mission

### Raw Source
- Headline: "Naturally San Diego is the community for the natural product industry in San Diego."
- Mission: "We aim to make greater San Diego the new nexus of natural products entrepreneurship."
- What we do: "We're not just another trade organization, we want to make dramatic changes in our CPG ecosystem and provide networking, education and resources for our members."
- Founded: Mid-2020 (first blog post June 2, 2020)

### Build-Ready Copy

**One-liner:**
San Diego's home for natural products founders — and everyone who helps them win.

**Mission statement (short form):**
We're building San Diego into the best place in the world to launch and grow a natural products brand. Through community, education, and connections that actually matter.

**Mission statement (long form / About page):**
Naturally San Diego launched in 2020 with a simple belief: San Diego has everything it takes to be the next great hub for natural products and CPG — the talent, the brands, the energy. What it needed was a community to pull it all together.

Five years later, that community is real. Founders, investors, retailers, and industry veterans showing up for each other — at pitch competitions, over coffee, in Slack, and at every event in between.

We're not a trade association. We're not a networking club. We're the connective tissue of San Diego's natural products ecosystem.

**Values (rewritten in brand voice):**
- Building an inclusive community — everyone from day-one founders to industry veterans
- Transparency with our members, sponsors, and partners — always
- Empowering the people who show up
- Staying curious — because the industry never stops moving
- Collaboration over competition — always
- Creativity and entrepreneurial spirit in everything we build
- Creating health and harmony between people and the planet

**1% for the Planet:**
Naturally San Diego is a certified nonprofit partner of 1% for the Planet.

## Events

### Naturally Rising (formerly Pitch Slam)

**Raw facts:**
- Renamed from "Pitch Slam" to "Naturally Rising" — announced February 5, 2025
- Annual event, held in spring
- 2021: 1st Annual Pitch Slam (April 8, 2021)
- 2022: 2nd Annual (April 7, 2022) — held at BRICK in Liberty Station
- 2023: Winner was Root and Splendor (Kristin Fracassi, Founder); MC was Ray Latif (BevNET Taste Radio); Finalists: I Amaranth, RationAle, Brixy, Cien Chiles, Wynn's Kitchen, Root and Splendor
- 2024: 35+ applicants, 6 finalists, 350-person audience, $100,000+ prize package
- 2022 winner Gear Hugger went on to win nationally (Naturally Network / New Hope competition)

**Build-Ready Copy:**

*Page headline:* Naturally Rising

*Subhead:* The stage is yours. Show us what you've built.

*Description:*
Every spring, Naturally San Diego opens the floor to San Diego's most promising natural products brands. Founders pitch live, on-stage, to a panel of expert judges and an audience of 350 — competing for a prize package worth $100,000+ in products and services.

This is Naturally Rising. It started in 2021 as the Pitch Slam. The name changed. The stakes didn't.

*Past winner highlight:*
2023 winner Kristin Fracassi of Root and Splendor took the stage and took the prize. In 2022, Gear Hugger went even further — winning the national Naturally Network competition at New Hope Expo East.

# Brand Identity — Naturally San Diego

> Extracted from the Naturally Network Brand Guide 2024. This file is the single source of truth for all brand-related decisions on the website.

## Mission

Naturally Network's mission is to harness, accelerate, and elevate the power and impact of conscious business practices in the natural products industry.

We empower conscious business through timely education, community-focused events, and inclusive programs designed to increase access to the industry.

---

## Core Values

### Community
We encourage and grow our community of conscious businesses regionally and as one national home. We believe in diversity and inclusion as a foundational value and priority in everything we do. We champion everyone from industry veterans to newcomers across the ecosystem who share the responsibility, desire, and goal of creating health and harmony between people and the planet.

### Collaboration
Collaboration is at the heart of what we do. Conscious business is the connective tissue across our network. We recognize our impact in shaping the future is exponential when shared.

### Transformation
We support industry transformation through a conscious business framework including:
- Passion
- Purpose
- People
- Planet
- Prosperity

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Use the primary full-color logo whenever possible
- Ensure it is always legible
- Available in CMYK and PMS versions (ai, eps, png, jpg)

### Logo Variants
- **Icon**: Standalone leaf icon — use on swag, presentations (primary logo should appear elsewhere)
- **Secondary Logo**: Vertical version with icon as separate element — use where vertical works best
- **White Logo**: Use over photographic or colored backgrounds where dark green wouldn't be legible
- **White Knockout Logo/Icon**: Background shows through the leaves and "Naturally" text
- **Single Color Green**: Go-to for single color printing
- **Single Color Black**: Only when printing in black/white

### Logo Don'ts
- Do NOT alter the logo in any way
- Do NOT use on clashing backgrounds
- Do NOT use on busy backgrounds
- Do NOT modify size/relationship of "Naturally" and Region name
- Do NOT enclose in a shape
- Do NOT add a drop shadow or stroke
